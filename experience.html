My Experience that forever I won't Forget

I. A Day With Us Program

The first experience that I can't forget is A Day With Us Program for our IMD314 Class Assignment. This Program was held in Sk Hamzah (1), Machang on 5th of January 2023. In this program we play with the library prefect students and introduce them to mobile library. It's an experience because we all in one class do it together with the help of our lecturer, Madam Sharliza Sapiai or Madam Zaza and it strenghten our relationship between each other.

II. A trip to Terengganu with a bunch of friends

Actually, the "A Day With Us Program" is our second option for IMD314. The first one is to take students from SK Bukit Tiu on a field trip to Terengganu science center. However that plan was scrapped because SK Bukit Tiu withdraw last minutes. It sadden us but on a good part, we enjoy exploring Terengganu. Went to Masjid Tenku Ampuan Zahimah to pray, attend online class in Kuala Terengganu mall. We even went to the famous Terengganu Drawbridge. After visiting the drawbridge, we watch Avatar on cinema. It was tiring but fun :DDDD
