My Education

Primary School

This is my first school (well I do go to kindegarden for two years but let just start with the formal one). This s Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Tasik Ampang, Selangor or SKTT for short. Quick info and history, this school was located in Jalan Tasek Tambahan 2, Ampang Selangor was built towards the end of 1983 and was finished in 1984. The school covers 5.5 acres and is made up of two one-story prefabricated building blocks. Originally, this structure had an office, a library, eleven classrooms, and two teachers' restrooms, but it lacked essential amenities such as electricity, running water, and a canteen. However, year by year, SKTT become one of the most competent school for the Hulu Langat district. My pops registered me to this school in 2009 and I have my education here for six years. From standard one until three, I enter class Cekal, on Standard four till six, I entered Fikir. I got 4B 1C for my UPSR (Ujian Penilaian Sekolah Rendah).
